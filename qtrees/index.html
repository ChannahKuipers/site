Qtrees

Qtrees is a application for the citizens in Berlin that are concerned about the health of street trees. By using this app, people can contribute and prefent the tree from drought by watering the trees together with other citizens in their neighborhood.

— See prototype

This project was an assignment for the subject Master Test during the international minor User Experience Design.

Learnings:
Working for a client, implementing the different knowledge learned during the minor into this final project.

The Problem

The client; Citylab is developing an intelligent watering prediction model that will optimise the watering of city trees in Berlin, named QTrees. There is not yet an application that transfers this data to the citizens. Trees need a lot of water, especially during the hot summer days. The municipality can not only do this. The citizens would be a good help to water the trees. The municipality therefore wants the residents to help water the trees in their neighbourhood and the Qtrees app would be the tool to make this happen.

Design Challenge

How can I make an application that citizens can use to contribute into the watering of street trees, so that the trees in the city stay healthy and hydrated?

Process

Target Group Analyse

The targetgroup: the citizens of Berlin, (young) adult (age 18+). They live in a big city and have busy lives with not much time to enjoy nature. When they do they appreciate it much more. They also do care about the health of the nature in the city and feel sad when they see neglected trees and plants.

Style & Moodboard

Colors and typography + a little moodboard used as insipration.

Scenario

1. The user sees on the homescreen of the app that one of the users trees is in need of water soon.

2. The user selects the tree and plans a watering meeting with other tree caretaker/parent, these people are for example: neighbors, co-workers, family members etc. depents on the location of your tree.

3. The user selects the days + time they are available. The day the most people (depents on how much water) for example minimum of 5 people are availible gets chosen to water the tree together on that day.

4. The user selects how much water they are bringing (bucket 10-20l, jerrycan 20l, watering can 10l). The user sees an idication how much water in collected for the meet up.

5. The user is at the meet up to water the tree. Every user has to confirm their watering.

6. After watering the user kan report a concern, when they see something could be wrong with the tree.

7. The user confirm the watering is done and has sent in a concern. The user gets a thank you message from the tree.

Outcome: Hifi prototype

Aspects to inhance the user experience and to keep the users engaged:

  • Personalization: personal account, choose your own tree and give it a name.
  • Social aspect: interaction with other users/people in the neighborhood.
  • Knowledge: learning about trees and their care.
  • Flexiblity: meet up for treewatering when it suits the user, no obligations.
  • Notifications: friendly reminders, keeping track of your trees, watering days and more.
